How to Use IFL — Service Contract Engine

The House of Kel LLC · CAGE 152U4 · SDVOSB · Full bid lifecycle: Discovery → Invoice

Step 1 — Discovery

Two ways to load opportunities. Paste: copy listings from SAM.gov or ESBD in TITLE | SOL# | DUE | AGENCY | LOCATION format and hit AI Triage — the engine scores each one GO / MARGINAL / PASS against your service lanes and set-aside profile. SAM Pull: enter keywords, NAICS, state, and set-aside then hit Pull SAM.gov Opportunities — your backend fetches live results directly from the SAM API and drops them into your list. Triage SAM Items to score the pulled batch. Select a GO contract and proceed.

Step 2 — Intake

Upload the SOW and Wage Determination PDFs. Copy the extraction prompt and run it through ChatGPT (AI Mode) with both files attached — it parses the entire solicitation and returns structured JSON. Paste the JSON output into the app and click Parse JSON. Every contract field auto-fills: scope, labor categories, hours, WD rates, risk flags, evaluation type (LPTA / Best Value). Review, edit anything off, log any amendments (A0001, A0002, etc.), set evaluation type, then confirm. The Compliance Checklist activates after confirmation — start checking items as you complete them.

Step 3 — Sourcing

AI generates 4–6 subcontractor candidates near the place of performance — staffing agencies, small subs, labor brokers. Each card shows a credibility score based on gov experience, years in business, and similar contracts. Small/local firms surface first. Click a card, confirm the bill rate, add years in business and gov contract count, then lock as Primary Sub. Set a Backup Sub from any other card or via manual entry — backup is required for new/unproven subs and strengthens your proposal. Hit Sub Onboarding Packet to generate a print-ready agreement, SCA flow-down notice, and insurance requirements doc for your locked sub.

Step 4 — Pricing

Bill rate and WD floor auto-load from intake and sourcing. Set overhead %, margin target, evaluation type, and pricing strategy (Aggressive / Balanced / Safe). Hit Calculate — you get a full breakdown: COGS, overhead, loaded rate, your government rate, gross profit per hour, margin %, total contract value, net to owner. The strategy band shows four price points (Floor / Aggressive / Balanced / Safe) and a TARGET WIN PRICE calibrated to your eval type and strategy. Use Past Award Lookup to query USASpending.gov for historical awards in your NAICS and state — click Apply to pre-fill the competitor range. A GO / MARGINAL / PASS verdict fires based on margin threshold and WD compliance. Confirm pricing to lock it.

Step 5 — Proposal

Fill in section-level details — technical approach, staffing plan, quality control, past performance, reps/certs. Click Generate Full Proposal — AI builds a complete SAM-style response document using all your contract data. The narrative is location-specific and agency-specific, not generic boilerplate. Output includes: cover page, Section B pricing schedule, Section C technical approach with your backup sub and prime oversight language, Section H management plan, Section K reps/certs, Section L past performance, a Risk Mitigation section (top 3 contract-specific risks), and a Why Us section tailored to this contract. Copy, download, or print.

Step 6 — Submit

The Win Readiness Score runs automatically — 10 checks across intake, sourcing, pricing, compliance, and proposal. Hard blocks (incomplete intake, no sub locked, below WD floor) prevent submission. Scores below 60% require confirmation. Upload all submission files — they get auto-named SOLID_Category_Filename.ext and stored in a folder for this solicitation. Download the full ZIP package when ready. Check your compliance score (target 80%+). Log submission details and hit Submit — the bid is recorded in your pipeline. The app resets for the next contract.

Step 7 — Invoice

After award, go to Step 7 to generate monthly invoices. Contract banner auto-populates from your locked bid data — SOL#, agency, your rate, subcontractor. Select the billing period (defaults to current month), enter actual hours worked that period, add any ODCs. Hit Generate — the invoice prints to a new window ready to submit to the government via IPP or email. Invoice history tracks all periods billed for this contract with running total. After generating, the period auto-advances to the next month. Remember: you also receive an invoice from your sub — pay them from what the government pays you. The spread is your margin.

Key Rules

  • WD floor is law. Your bill rate must be at or above the SCA wage floor. Violating it = DOL audit, back wages, debarment.
  • 1099s on SCA-covered work = illegal. Workers must be W-2. Get it in writing in your sub agreement.
  • Always set a backup sub. Evaluators want to see contingency. A call-out with no backup = cure notice.
  • Net-to-owner is the real number. Not top-line contract value. GP/hr × hours = what you actually make.
  • LPTA = price wins. Best Value = price + narrative wins. Know which one you're in before you price.
  • Amendments must be acknowledged. Missing one amendment = automatic disqualification.
  • CPARS builds your future. Every contract is a past performance reference. Do the work right.
  • SCA compliance = certified payroll. Keep records 3 years. DOL can audit any time.
  • VA + SDVOSB = your lane. Fort Cavazos is backyard. Work outward from there.
